Inova is a newly formed company that aims to create social value and economic growth through frugal innovation. The core elements of frugal innovation is reducing costs and increasing economic growth, while making sure our projects are sustainable for the environment and the local community. We work with different companies in developing countries to create quality products and solutions that are affordable and durable.

Frugal innovation is a type of innovation in which technological products are customised at low prices and high volumes in and for emerging markets (Dutta and Benavente, 2011).

We are hosting innovation camps around Norway to help local businesses create new frugal solutions. Please check our Schedule page for upcoming innovation camps.
